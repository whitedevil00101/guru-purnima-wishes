गुरु पूर्णिमा

The sacred festival honoring our teachers

ॐ

Guru Purnima is traditionally celebrated to honor one's chosen spiritual teachers. On this day, disciples offer puja (worship) to their guru. This sacred day falls on the full moon day (Purnima) in the Hindu month of Ashadha (July-August).
